# STRUCTURA · Core — Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Tagline:** *Structural Intelligence for Institutional Markets*

**Positioning:** STRUCTURA · Core is a professional-grade market analysis observatory. The brand conveys precision, mathematical rigor, and institutional trust—not retail trading or speculation.

## Logo

### Primary Mark: Golden Spiral

The STRUCTURA logo is based on the **mathematical Golden Spiral (φ = 1.618...)**, symbolizing natural market geometry and structural intelligence.

**Logo Concept:**
- Golden spiral emanating from center
- Fibonacci ratio intersection points as nodes
- Outer boundary circle representing market scope
- Cross-axis lines showing geometric precision

**Usage:**
- Animated version for web/digital
- Static SVG for print and social

### Logo Color
- **Primary:** `hsl(168, 35%, 45%)` — Muted Emerald/Teal
- **Glow nodes:** `hsl(168, 35%, 55%)`

## Visual Language

### Design Principles

1. **Institutional, Not Retail**
   - Cold, precise, observatory-grade aesthetic
   - Bloomberg Terminal meets CERN control room
   - No gamification, no bright neon

2. **Data Density Over White Space**
   - Information-rich displays
   - Terminal-style panels
   - Grid-based layouts

3. **Mathematical Precision**
   - Geometric patterns (spirals, grids)
   - Formula-first presentations
   - Explicit constraints and boundaries

### Do's
- ✓ Use dark backgrounds with muted accents
- ✓ Display formulas and mathematical notation
- ✓ Use monospace fonts for data
- ✓ Show geometric structures (spirals, grids)
- ✓ Maintain high information density

### Don'ts
- ✗ Bright colors or neon aesthetics
- ✗ Trading signals or predictions
- ✗ Retail-style marketing language
- ✗ Gamification elements
- ✗ Excessive animations

## Brand Voice

### Tone
- **Precise** — Every word matters
- **Neutral** — Descriptive, not prescriptive
- **Technical** — Speaks to professionals
- **Understated** — Authority without boasting

### Vocabulary

**Use:**
- "Structure" not "Pattern"
- "Context" not "Signal"
- "Observation" not "Prediction"
- "Geometric" not "Technical"
- "Volatility-adjusted" not "Normalized"

**Avoid:**
- Signals, Predictions, Opportunities
- Bullish/Bearish directional language
- Trading jargon (buy/sell, entry/exit)
- Marketing superlatives
